Abstract

Text rendering has recently emerged as one of the most challenging frontiers in visual generation. However, text editing within images remains largely unexplored. We introduce TextEditBench, a comprehensive benchmark emphasizing reasoning-intensive editing scenarios.

Unlike traditional benchmarks, we propose a Dual-Track Evaluation Framework combining pixel-level objective metrics with MLLM-based semantic expectations (SE). Our experiments demonstrate that current SOTA models still struggle with tasks requiring multi-step reasoning and contextual understanding.
